Bottle makes scientific research easier to understand. Whether you're breaking into a new field or have never touched a paper before, we're making scientific information accessible to everyone!

Scientific papers are hard to read,
and that's by design.

Scientific papers are designed to quickly communicate information between experts in the field. To accomplish this, they use field-specific jargon, focus on heavy mathematical derivations, and lack important context—all of which create a barrier to understanding for everyone else.

No scalable medium exists to communicate scientific research to non-experts.

So we've created a platform meant for you.y ou

Bottle is an alternative to traditional scientific papers—a platform for scientific information in a format anyone can understand. We source translations of scientific papers, written for the general public, from researchers in the field. Then, we organize these translations (along with other useful information, such as definitions) into a node-based knowledge graph that shows the relationship between pieces of information.

Credible

We source translations from experts at accreddited institutions, ideally the author of the original paper or study, so you can trust what you're reading.

We're experimenting with AI translations, but these will be clearly marked as such.

Straightforward

We drop the jargon and heavy derivations to focus on the intuition and core ideas behind each paper.

We use analogies and well-known language to convey ideas, as if you're having a casual conversation with the expert.

Context-Driven

Visually exploring the relationships between content enables you to fit new information into your preexisting knowledge map.

That way, you can understand what you're reading in the context of your experiences.
